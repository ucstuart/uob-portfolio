About Me


Professional Memberships

Stuart was admitted to the Institute of Analysts and Progammers with the grade of Member on the 8th April 1998, you can see more about the institution and it work here: https://iap.org.uk. The institute has a number of grades of membership which include (listed in senority): Licentiate, Graduate, Associate Member, Member and Fellow. To become a Fellow requires invitation by the Institution and is only done in exceptional cases.


Professional Work

Stuart has worked through his long career in Informational Technology and Telecommunications, having built up a number of businesses and have sold them on. In 2013 Stuart commenced Initial IT Services Ltd, and decided to go back to his roots of Telecommunications, and support the Reseller Channel by providing wholesale and distribution services to the channel. This includes wholesale SIP, our own MVNO offering UC-Mobile, international distribution deals with Grandstream, Vodia Networks, Jusan Telecommuications and PBX Monitor as well as creating solutions that allow our Resellers to be different in the crowded marketplace.


Professional Interests

Stuart is interested in creating solutions within the Telecommunications industry that are innvoative, and enable our resellers to be different in the crowded marketplace. He also has a keen interest in Telecommunications Regulations, including Ofcom Regulations, Number Porting, General Conditions, Telephone Preference Service (TPS/CTPS). Stuart demonstrates a fountain of knowledge, including caselaw, in relation to Information Technology, Website Accessibility, and Telecommunications as well as general Civil and Contract Law. The interest in website accessibility came from his work with Julie Howell at the RNIB on the project "Websites that Work".
